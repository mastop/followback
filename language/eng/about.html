About Followback

When a developer has nothing to do, creates "things" like Followback.

A totally free system that automatically follow people on twitter, find people related to their search by a keyword. The system also allows you to debug who and how many people follow and still give Unfollow those who do not automatically follow you.

What are the advantages of using Followback?

  • You can use the Followback many times as desired.
  • Imagine arriving and not knowing anyone. In that case, you could create a list and start interacting.
  • Later, you can debug who and how many people you follow and still Unfollow those who do not automatically follow you.
  • The system is totally free!
  • Increasing your number of followers.
  • Better interaction and use of Twitter.
