API and Requests

What's API?

It is an inteface that allows Followback to communicate with twitter, responsible for allowing you to follow and get followers using our system.

Which is the API limit requests?

The limit requests twitter API is 350 requests per hour. Every hour the twitter resets these requests allowing you to make 350 new requests.

What's requests?

Requisition is nothing more than a query to the information from twitter, a query is made ​​on the basis of data from twitter. About, users, tweets, among other things.

How does it works?

I need to register?

To use the system, you do not need to perform any kind of registration on Followback. You only need to login with your Twitter account in dev.twitter.com, and create an "application", so you get two tokens, the Consumer Key and Consumer Secret, which will allow you to use the Followback.

How do i get new followers?

Just do a search by keywords and the system will automatically follow people who tweeted about it, so when the system start following these people, there is the possibility of them follow you back.

How many times i can use this?

You can use as many times as you want, just respecting the limit of requests twitter!

How to unfollow who don't follow me back?

To unfollow the people who do not follow you back, just select the number of people who want to stop following and click Unfollow, the system will automatically unfollow people who do not follow back.

Exist a limit of unfollows?

There is a limit to unfollows each time you use so that the process does not slow down, but you can repeat the process sometimes, just respecting the limit of requests on twitter.

I can know who i am unfollow?

No, you can not know who is giving unfollow, but after the process unfollow the system will show you whom you failed to follow.

How do i search?

A search of the keywords must be relevant content, content that you like, or talk about.
